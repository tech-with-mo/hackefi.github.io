Installation Guide

How do you use this EFI? You probably know but I´ll explain it anyway.

Plug your USB in that contains your EFI Partition. If you want to Paste this Directly on your SSD (which I advise you against) remember to make a Backup.

Next up Mount the EFI Partition with a Handy tool called ESP Mounter Pro.

Identify your EFI and click "Mount".

Open Finder and navigate to the Volume called EFI. Copy your current EFI and Paste it somewhere else. Go back and Delete the EFI. Grab my EFI from my GitHub and double click it. macOS will automatically extract the zip. Grab the EFI Folder and Cut it from there to your EFI Volume. You shouldn't move it because something might go wrong during the process so cutting is safer.

Once that's done unmount using ESP Mounter Pro. Shutdown your Hack. Reboot of the USB and if it boots repeat the same Process as before but copy the EFI from your USB to your Main Drive. That's it!
